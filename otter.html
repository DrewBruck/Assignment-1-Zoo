OTTER

Otters are playful, carnivorous, semi-aquatic animals found on every continent except Antarctica and Australia. There are 13 species in total. Their webbed feet and powerful tails make them very strong swimmers, along with their special fur that keeps them insulated in water. River otters primarily feed on aquatic organisms such as fish, frogs, crayfish, turtles, insects, and some small mammals. Although they typically live in pairs, they are extremely social creatures and often socialize in groups as well.
