OTTER

Otters are playful, carnivorous, semi- aquatic animals found in every continent except Antartica and Australia. There are 13 species in total. Their webbed feet and powerful tails make them very strong swimmers, along with their special fur that keeps them sealed from water. River otters enjoy eating mostly aquatic organisms including fish, frogs, crayfish, turtles, insects and some small mammals.
